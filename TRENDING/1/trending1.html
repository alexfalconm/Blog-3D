This high poly of a scope is a #WIP by Ryzin’s Dan Kenton. A friend of his, who is a concept artist, wanted to send him
something that would be great practice for them both. Dan then turned his concept into a game-ready piece. Dan says
“the most enjoyable part was being able to import his sculpt to use as direct reference. It makes the modeling process
go by much quicker just being able to have the concept in 3D in the same scene. The most challenging aspect was
getting proper topology. As the original concept was sculpted in Zbrush, it can sometimes be hard to replicate different
form changes and such in poly modeling. The next stage of the process would be to perfect this high poly model. As it
stands right now, some of the edges are a bit too soft and it has some shading errors in places. The next step also includes
deciding what materials I will want to assign during the texturing phase, as this will decide edge widths in the high poly.”

Artist: Dan Kenton

Renders: Alan Van Ryzin
